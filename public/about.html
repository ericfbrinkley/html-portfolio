About Me

With over 20 years of experience in the esports industry, I am a passionate and results-driven leader who excels in operations management, business development, live event production, sponsorship and influencer marketing, and strategic partnerships. I hold an MBA in Organizational Leadership from Santa Clara University, where I honed my skills in strategic planning, financial analysis, and team building.

As the Director of Programs & Content at the Global Esports Federation, I lead the development, growth, and execution of the Global Esports Tour and Global Esports Cups properties, which aim to create emotional connections with athletes and fans alike, enhancing their experience of competitive play. I also spearhead strategic partnerships with AAA and AA game publishers, securing IP rights and providing additional revenue streams to GEF programs and initiatives. Previously, I held significant roles in top-tier organizations, such as FACEIT Media and Rally.io, where I spearheaded the development of several groundbreaking esports IPs, both independently and as co-owned properties, which have made a significant impact in the industry.
